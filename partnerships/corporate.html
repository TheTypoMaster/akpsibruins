Corporate Partnerships

The Premier Business Fraternity | UCLA Alpha Upsilon Chapter

Thank you for your interest in partnering with the Alpha Upsilon chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi, the premier co-ed professional business fraternity at the University of California, Los Angeles. As one of the most distinguished business organizations on campus, Alpha Kappa Psi can serve as a liaison between your company and the students of UCLA. Consisted of ambitious, goal-oriented students and having relationships with various professional organizations, we can help your company strengthen its presence at UCLA, as well as assist in effectively finding a concentration of the most qualified candidates for employment. Our partnership can range from displaying your company link and logo on our website and rush events to co-hosting workshops, career fairs, case study competitions.

In the past, we have hosted case competitions with Intel, case workshops with Deloitte, and networking events with Target. With our involvement and experience in a wide range of campus events, we can help you reach out to diverse students through any method of your choice.

How do I become a sponsor?

The resources, experience, and members Alpha Kappa Psi can provide for your company are valuable towards building a meaningful relationship. Please download the condensed Corporate Partnership Package and contact us to further discuss our sponsorship packages or a custom package that is right for your company. We look forward to working with you.
