A premier organization of ambitious and skilled student leaders making an enduring difference in the community

Who We Are

Alpha Upsilon Consulting (AUC) is a student consulting group founded by Alpha Kappa Psi, Alpha Upsilon Chapter. Established during the Winter of 2007, AUC is a philanthropy program seeking to offer long term benefits to the community by assisting local companies and non-profit organizations. AUC offers pro-bono consulting services in strategy, management, marketing, technology, finance, and human resources.

Our Mission

To assist promising organizations by providing long lasting, creative, and effective business solutions utilizing the professional and analytical talents of the Alpha Upsilon Chapter.

Our Goal

We are not motivated by financial gain--rather, we wish to gain experience within the consulting field by working to help local businesses and non-profit organizations develop and achieve the same level of success as private organizations.

Our Operations

We aim to help organizations through improved marketing, strategic development, and financial planning. Our operations, stylized around professional firms, involve the isolation of critical business problems, conducting research and analysis, and the presentation of practical business solutions.
