Message From The President


We are proud to say that Alpha Kappa Psi is the world's oldest and largest professional fraternity spanning all geographies, careers, and backgrounds. As a brother, you will have access to numerous resources and incredible professional and academic guidance throughout and beyond college years. Many of our members have moved on to work as invesment bankers, consultants, advertisers, doctors, lawyers, and entrepreneurs.

The unifying factor for all of our alumni is that they can all attribute their success to the foundations which Alpha Kappa Psi has established. We encourage you to join our brotherhood to make lifelong friends and to experience the professional benefits we have to offer.

Sincerely,

Willy Huang
President | Alpha Kappa Psi, Alpha Upsilon
