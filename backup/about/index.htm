About
Alpha Kappa Psi

Alpha Kappa Psi is the world's oldest, largest, and most decorated professional fraternity, with a network spanning across the globe consisting of all fields and backgrounds. Our main purpose is to provide our brothers with professional guidance during their college career to ensure that after graduating, they will have both the professional skills that resonate with whatever industry they join and vast network of lifelong friends to turn to. Through professional workshops including resume building and interview fine-tuning, mentorship programs, philanthropy efforts, fun filled social events, Alpha Kappa Psi is the premier developer of principled business leaders. With alumni including past Presidents and many Fortune 500 CEO's, we invite you to join our distinguished brotherhood and lifelong experience that is Alpha Kappa Psi.

Alpha Upsilon Chapter

The original Alpha Upsilon Chapter at the University of California Los Angeles was originally established in 1926. However, after nearly half a century of success, Alpha Kappa Psi at UCLA was disbanded due to the Vietnam War in the 1970's.

During the Winter Quarter in 1999, a group of ambitious students took it upon themselves to re-establish the Alpha Upsilon Chapter. Assisted by the Alpha Zeta Chapter at the University of Southern California, the first 25 members of the reborn Alpha Upsilon chapter were pinned by Ms. Tammy Lu, a representative from the National organization. From there, a new legacy was written.
