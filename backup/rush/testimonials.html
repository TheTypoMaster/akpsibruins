Testimonials
Not sure what Alpha Kappa Psi can do for you? See what current actives and alumni have to say!

"Alpha Kappa Psi has given me a sense of self confidence which I once lacked that has helped me improve my social networking skills and my ability to operate within a business environment. Outside of helping me with business, AKPsi has given me the opportunity to meet some of the closest friends I will ever make and establish meaningful relationships that will last forever." – David Lopez

"Coming into college I thought I had wanted to be an investment banker, not because I had known what the job entailed but because of the beautiful six digit salary. After pledging AKPsi, I was informed about the difficult lifestyle and hard work I would be subject to, but simultaneously I was encouraged to pursue such a challenging and rewarding career. Since surrounding myself with ambitious and likeminded individuals I've carved out a definite path for my future and am constantly motivated by those who've already been where I am today." – Kevin Chanthasiriphan

"Alpha Kappa Psi has been crucial in the development of my leadership skills as it has given me opportunities, previously inaccessible as an ordinary undergraduate student at UCLA. This organization is truly notable for its ability to transform those with the motivation and desire to succeed into business leaders. For the years that I have been a part of Alpha Kappa Psi, it has proven to be a learning ground where I am able to push myself and those around me; I have made many mistakes and I have learned, where making similar mistakes in a professional working environment would have been embarrassing and detrimental to my success." – Abraham Chon

"Life at UCLA can be really hectic, and without people to keep you grounded you can really lose a sense of what you want to do with your life and your future. Alpha Kappa Psi provided the foundation I needed to grow as a person, as a brother, and as a future entrepreneur." - Aston Tsui

"This fraternity introduced to me a network of individuals that I wouldn't have otherwise known. Now, I'm happy to call them my friends and brothers. Knowing them and being a part of chapter has made me more driven professionally and has made my college experience fuller and more rewarding." – Kim Phan

"My decision to join Alpha Kappa Psi was solidified at the first day of rush. Everybody in college strives to find a niche in order to survive. I found college students who enjoyed going out and living every single minute of life to its fullest; I also discovered individuals who were just as ambitious as I was. Joining Alpha Kappa Psi has given me the ability to exceed in ways I could never imagine – personally and career-wise." – Daniel Su

"I think that the connections that I have made in Alpha Kappa Psi are invaluable. The people that I have met, both actives and alumni, have connections in every field I could have ever imagined, and they are all willing to guide me in whatever I need help with." - Sonal Patel
