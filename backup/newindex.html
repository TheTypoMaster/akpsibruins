Welcome to Alpha Kappa Psi...
Hello and thank you for your interest in Alpha Kappa Psi. We are proud to say that Alpha Kappa Psi is the world's oldest and largest professional fraternity, spanning across all geographies, careers and backgrounds. As a brother, you will have access to numerous resources and incredible professional and academic guidance throughout and beyond your college years. Many of our members move on to work as investment bankers, consultants, advertisers, doctors, lawyers, and entrepreneurs.

The unifying factor for all of our alumni is that they can all attribute their success to the foundations which Alpha Kappa Psi has established. We encourage you to join our brotherhood to make lifelong friends and to experience the professional benefits we have to offer.

Come out the first week of Spring Quarter to our rush to learn what Alpha Kappa Psi can offer you.

Sincerely,

Beau Hoover
