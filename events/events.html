In partnership with UCLA's Alpha Kappa Psi:



Boeing's 2014 UCLA vs. USC Case Competition



Challenge yourself with an aerospace industry inspired business case and compete with your classmates and cross-town rivals to win the chance to network with Boeing executives, tour the facilities, and get the inside scoop on job opportunities!

This year's case study scenario focuses on the commercial satellite services market and will test your finance, market research, and global strategy skills.

8 teams will be selected (3-5 participants per team). Business majors preferred; all majors and all undergraduate class standings welcome.

Selected teams are required to attend the on-campus Kick-Off Event where the case and relevant details will be provided.

Preliminary presentations will be judged by Boeing executives on UCLA Campus.

Winners from each school will make a final presentation to a panel of Boeing senior executives at Boeing's El Segundo site. Both teams will be invited to a satellite production tour as well as an exclusive networking luncheon with executives.
