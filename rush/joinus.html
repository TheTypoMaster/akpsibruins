Dear Rushee,

Take the time and think of where you will be in five years time. You may find it difficult to imagine--and rightly so. Your time here at UCLA and how you make use of it can take you down an infinite number of courses. At times the course you travel may appear uneasy and out of your control. Alpha Kappa Psi helps its members brave the storm, steer clear of the fog, and adjust their sail to a secure future.

TESTIMONIALS


  • Coming into UCLA as a third-year transfer student, I wanted to surround myself with like-minded students that could motivate and mentor me. The brothers of Alpha Kappa Psi exceeded my expectations with their expansive training, number of opportunities, and wide network. When I applied for my first accounting internship, I was competing against over two hundred applicants but an Alpha Kappa Psi alumni within the company reached out to me and prepped me for the interview. If it wasn't for his help I would not have gotten the job. I know that the network I have gained through Alpha Kappa Psi is something that will stay with me post-graduation, and I am excited to transition from college into the work field with all the friends I have made through this organization.



    LUBINA KIM
    ZETA PI PLEDGE CLASS

  • I came into AKPsi looking for professional opportunities. I felt like everyone knew where they were going and I was definitely lost in my career goals. I got exactly what I wanted from AKPsi. They showed me every aspect and industry of business I needed to learn. I was able to go through what I didn't want, and finally find something I was sure and passionate about. Not only did this extensive network guide me to where I wanted to be, they got me there too. I am currently working at my alumnus's company.

    Something I didn't expect from them, however, means so much more to me now. College is about the people you meet, and the experiences you have. I met amazing people and shared with them some of my greatest memories. You don't really realize it, but when you call these 50+ people your brothers, they really are your brothers. These are the people who will study with you, help you prep for an interview, go to the gym with you, walk to class with you, who will just bet here for you when you need it. Having someone there for you that you can rely on is amazing. Having 50+ is harder to believe. I never expected to have all my roommates be from AKPsi and my best friends be from it either. I didn't expect it, but I wouldn't have had it any other way.



    ANDY NGUYEN
    ZETA NU PLEDGE CLASS



  • "The way you add value to your life is to devote ourselves to doing something that gives you purpose and meaning." AKPsi has certainly played a major role in my college experiences. The life-long friendships, valuable advice, mentors and brothers I have gained and met will remain a crucial part of my life. I'm proud to say that AKPsi is one of my greatest achievements at UCLA.



    BAILU ZHAN
    ZETA MU PLEDGE CLASS

  • Coming into college, everyone told me that I would find my closest friends here. However, after my first two quarters at UCLA, I still felt lost and alone in such a large school. I had no idea what I wanted to do and I did not have that close group of people whom I could call my best friends - until I decided to rush Alpha Kappa Psi.

    Now, after being in Alpha Kappa Psi for almost two years now, I have gained much more than a few new friends, but an actual family that I can count on for anything and everything, people that I can call my true brothers. My best memories and all of my achievements in college can be attributed to Alpha Kappa Psi and I can honestly say that I really would not be where I am in life without them.



    CAMILLE SU
    ZETA OMICRON PLEDGE CLASS

  • AKPsi has been an integral part of my college experience. The brothers in this chapter - my brothers - are my family here at UCLA. This chapter has been my home away from home for the past 3 years, and I've forged some of the most incredible memories with them. From learning how to unicycle, to shredding the slopes at Lake Tahoe and Big Bear, and to spontaneous trips to taco trucks at 2AM, AKPsi has given me everything I've wanted out of a group of friends at UCLA.

    Not only has the fraternity given me the brotherhood that I was looking for in college, AKPsi has helped me tremendously in terms of professional development. We have mentors, both active brothers and alumni, in almost every industry I can think of. It was because of them that I landed my first technical internship and now, my full-time offer. I honestly could not be where I am today without this chapter. And I truly can't imagine what my college experience would have been like without AKPsi.



    DAVID MAO
    ZETA LAMBDA PLEDGE CLASS
